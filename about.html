About Bohemian Forest

Bohemian Forest is a project born from a deep admiration for one of Central Europe’s most enchanting landscapes — the Šumava mountains, known internationally as the Bohemian Forest. This vast region of forests, rivers, peat bogs, and mountain meadows stretches across the Czech Republic, Germany, and Austria. For centuries, it has been a natural border, a refuge, and a source of inspiration for artists, travelers, and those seeking peace in nature.

Our mission is to share the essence of Šumava — not only through beautiful photography, but through stories, history, and reflections that reveal what makes this region so unique. The Bohemian Forest is more than a place on a map; it is a living ecosystem, a cultural landscape shaped by generations, and a symbol of coexistence between people and nature.

Through this website, we aim to build a bridge between past and present. You’ll find articles about traditional villages and crafts, guides for hiking and exploring hidden places, as well as insights into the forest’s wildlife and ecology. We also highlight local communities and their sustainable efforts to preserve this fragile environment for future generations.

Whether you are planning your first visit to Šumava or you already know its trails by heart, Bohemian Forest invites you to slow down, breathe deeply, and rediscover the simple beauty of untouched nature. Our hope is that every story and every image here inspires you to connect more deeply with the landscape — and perhaps to protect it, in your own way.

Ultimately, Bohemian Forest is not just about the region itself, but about a way of seeing the world: quietly, mindfully, and with respect for the places that still remain wild.

The Bohemian Forest has always been a land of quiet contrasts — dense spruce woods opening into sunlit clearings, misty valleys leading up to rugged ridges, and moments of stillness interrupted only by the rustle of wind or the call of a distant bird. It’s a place where time seems to move differently, shaped more by the rhythm of seasons than by the pace of modern life. In this landscape, simplicity becomes a kind of wisdom — reminding us that beauty often lives in silence, in patience, and in the unnoticed details of the natural world.

We believe that sharing this spirit through images and stories can help others rediscover a connection that modern life has almost forgotten. The Bohemian Forest teaches humility — it asks us to listen rather than to conquer, to observe rather than to consume. Every trail, stream, and ancient tree holds traces of the people and creatures who have called this forest home, weaving a story that continues to this day. By walking these paths, we become part of that story too.

Our vision for Bohemian Forest is to create more than just a website — we want to build a living archive of experiences, knowledge, and emotions linked to this extraordinary region. A space where travelers, photographers, writers, and locals can come together to share their perspectives, learn from one another, and contribute to the preservation of this landscape. Because the forest’s future depends on awareness — and awareness begins with wonder.
